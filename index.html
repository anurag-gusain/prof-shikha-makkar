Dr. Shikha Makkar is an Educationist, Researcher, Learner, & has been teaching undergraduate and postgraduate students for over 18 years. She is teaching as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Commerce, Ram Lal Anand College, University of Delhi. She is a Ph. D. Research Supervisor, at the Department of Commerce, University of Delhi. In order to broaden her research perspectives she recently completed the Summer Faculty Research Fellow programme (SFRF: 2025), at the Department of Management Studies, IIT Delhi. With a design Patent to her credit, she has published research papers in Scopus Indexed and UGC listed Journals and presented various research articles at international and national conferences and has also to her credit the best research paper award at various international conferences.

She is a Commerce graduate and scholarship Awardee, from Jesus and Mary College and Masters in Commerce from University of Delhi. She has completed her Ph.D. in the field of Organizational Behavior from the Department of Commerce, University of Delhi on the topic "Measurement of Spirituality and its impact on students and teachers". She has also Contributed in the e-PG Pathshala Project by UGC under the NMEICT mission of MHRD, Govt. of India, as Co-Author in Commerce subject Modules, on Management Concept and Organizational Behavior (Emotions & Leadership: An introduction) and Human Resource Management (Recruitment-Factors Affecting Recruitment). She is also invited as a Guest speaker/ moderator, and Resource Person for Faculty Development Programs on topics/discussions in the domain of Organizational Behavior, Human Resource Management, Spirituality & Research, by Management B-Schools, Institutions etc.
